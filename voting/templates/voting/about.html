Key Features

Secure Registration

Voter registration with ID upload, face capture, and security questions. Admin verification ensures only eligible voters participate.

End‑to‑End Encryption

Votes are encrypted on your device using RSA-2048 before transmission, ensuring that only the final tally is decrypted.

Real‑Time Analytics

Live turnout updates, heat maps of voter locations, and replay of vote flow provide unprecedented transparency.

Voter Receipt

Each vote generates a unique receipt ID and QR code, allowing voters to verify their vote was counted without revealing their choice.

AI‑Powered Security

Anomaly detection identifies suspicious patterns (e.g., rapid votes, IP concentration) and logs them for admin review.

Leader Accountability

After elections, voters can rate leaders based on their manifesto promises, and leaders receive feedback via a dedicated dashboard.

Our Journey

2024 – Conceptualization

Idea born to digitize student elections at MMUST, focusing on security and transparency.

2025 – Pilot Launch

First pilot run for School of Computing elections with 500+ voters and zero security incidents.

2026 – Full Deployment

University‑wide rollout with real‑time analytics, live maps, and AI‑powered security features.

Future – Continuous Improvement

Expanding to staff elections, integrating with national ID systems, and adopting post‑quantum cryptography.
